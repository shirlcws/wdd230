We Have a story to Share!

Launched in 2023, Bountiful Fresh is fast becoming one of the areas largest growing direct-to-consumer organic fruits and vegetable suppliers in the Carlsbad coastal area.

Before lauching Bountiful Fresh, our founder and CEO Rodger Hurley was an organic farmer. When his boys took over the farm, Roger had a lot of free time, and was feeling the need to try something new.

His experience as a farmer of organic fruits and vegetables made him desire to help customers get the fruits and vegetables his sons produce, to the table in a shorter amount of time.
