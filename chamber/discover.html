Blackfoot Tribe

The first settlers in the area were the Blackfoot Native Americans.
The population is estmated at 12,322.

Potato Museum

One of the town's claim to fame is our Potato Museum located inside the old train station. Out of towner's get a souvenier sack of potatos to take home!

Snake River

A major water source for the area is the Snake River.

Yellowstone National Park

A major tourist attraction for anyone visiting the area.

The Brown Mansion

Built in 1905, is a must see on the list of the Historical Registry.
