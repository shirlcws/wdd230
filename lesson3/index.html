SITE PURPOSE:

The Chamber offers proven network support for local business owners, allowing them a way to promote their products and services to the community. There are different benefit packages set up to entice varying membership levels for members through joining and participating in the Greater Blackfoot Chamber of Commerce.

SCENARIOS

A business owner or enterprenier who is either new to the area or just wants to meet other business owners in the area to find potential clients or entroduce his or her field of expertise.
