Blackfoot Chamber News and Events

SITE PURPOSE:

The Chamber offers proven network support for local business owners, allowing them a way to promote their products and services to the community. There are different benefit packages set up to entice varying membership levels for members through joining and participating in the Greater Blackfoot Chamber of Commerce.

SCENARIOS

A business owner or enterprenier who is either new to the area or just wants to meet other business owners working in the vicinity to find networking oportunities that could serve to benefit all parties or clients.

Business parties are able to introduce themselves in a networking setting, to share their fields of expertise to other business owners who might want to reciprocate.

Content and Development Resources

My goal is to increase awareness of the benefits a membership with the Blackfot chamber of Commerce can do for a new or existing business in the area. A high priority would be to offer varying levels of memberships so no one is left out, because they can't afford the fees.
